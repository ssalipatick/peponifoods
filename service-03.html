This account is available to the account owner "on demand" and is available for frequent and immediate access by the account owner.

Again depending on the Sacco that maintains the account may charge the account holder maintenance or transaction fees or offer the service free to the holder and charge only if the holder uses an add-on service such as an overdraft.

Sacco’s offering transactional accounts may allow an account to go into overdraft if that has been previously arranged. If an account has negative balance money is being borrowed from the Sacco and interest and overdraft fees as normally charged.

Using the Account:

Members get the following services through their mobile phones;

.

1. Balance enquiry - Members can enquire their account balances e.g Savings, Loans, Shares, deposits.

.

2. Withdrawal - Allows member to make withdrawals from their current accounts

.

3. Balance enquiry - Members can enquire their account balances e.g Savings, Loans, Shares, deposits.

.

4. Loan Repayment - This service allows members to repay their Sacco loans.

.

5. Mini-statement - Members can get a mini-statement for the last 5 transactions

.

6. Change PIN - Members can change their PIN numbers as they wish

.

7. Inter-Sacco transfers(members of different Sacco’s)

.

8. In-Sacco transfers(members in the same Sacco)

.

Adding Funds to Your Account

When it comes time to contribute money to your current Sacco account, you will take one of the following steps:

Sacco- money agents or field agents :

Using the vast SACCO agent network a member deposits cash through the agent allowing members to deposit cash outside of SACCO working hours or at a location that’s more convenient for them.

Deposit checks:

You can deposit checks directly into a current account. When you make the deposit, just put your Sacco bank account number on the deposit slip.Then present the reciept to your sacco premises

peer-peer(member based):

You can recieve funds from another member whether from the same sacco or a another one.
