Services offered by Peponi foods


Market Place platform.

Marketplace is a platform where consumers (urban restaurants, hotels & homes) place their orders. Marketplace has a list of items that peponi brings to the market for.

Katumwa platform:

Katumwa is an on-demand logistics platform where we buy un-used inventory(goods space) at fairly good prices from commuter taxis and empty cargo trucks on road from rural to urban centers and we deploy that infrastructure as a cost-efficient and flexible agricultural transportation network.

Sacco-Pay

Peponi will have a digitized saving and credit cooperative (SACCO) that will be authorized to take deposits from and lend to its members. Sacco-pay is mobile banking platform for peponi SACCO which will enable members to transact through their mobile phones at the comfort of their work places and homes. Farmers, commuter taxi drivers and the final consumers will be registered for this service automatically.
